Tsimtsum is the creation of [[2. Main Theoretical Pillars/Theory Notes/Weiss-citation-empty-space\|the Empty Space]] which was formed when [[3. Concepts/Eyn Sof\|Eyn Sof]] retracted from itself in order to [[3. Concepts/Turning Around\|re-]]enter it with order. If the realm of [[3. Concepts/tohu va vohu\|tohu va vohu]] was undifferentiated, which was chaos, retraction was the first necessary step for the emergence of order, or differentiation between aspects.
