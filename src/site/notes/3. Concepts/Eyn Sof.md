<div style="text-align: right;">
_Eyn Sof_<br>
</div>

_eyn_ (אין) = "there is no" / _sof_ (סוף) = "end"

[[2. Main Theoretical Pillars/Theory Notes/historical-kabbalah-and-philosophy\|A stand-in term denoting infinity, the absence of beginning or end, or perhaps a supreme entity, echoing Aristotelian thought on the 'primal cause' or 'unmoved mover.']]
