"The beginning is embedded in the end, and the end in the beginning." [^1]

The cadence of time is discerned as the encircling line of the future that continuously unpacks all that was contained in the impenetrable point of the past. (22, retroactive not yet)


[^1]: Sefer Yetzirah 1:7
