R. Nachman therefore postulates that only the mystic zaddik, who is of the aspect of Moses, the greatest of the mystics, who knows both the secret of silence from the speech of the intellect, and the secret of the mystical melody of belief in ‘the light of ‘Eyn Sof,’ is capable of engaging the heresy that comes from the void, contending with it, and elevating the souls fallen into its depths. 

The person who worships exclusively through wisdom and speech is incapable of contending with the void that lacks any intellect or language. Only one who has acquired the ability of silence and the casting off of the intellect on the one hand and, on the other, ‘knows melody,’41 is able to believe and to play the melody of the presence of ‘the light of ‘Eyn Sof’ when faced with the void.

The belief required in confronting the void has two components: silence and melody. Silence is the relinquishing of the intellect, and parallels the ‘casting off of the intellect.’ (Mysticism and Madness, 164)

wolfson shows that messianic time is bridged in the middle in the act of rectification
weiss describes the messianic state as a song that harmonises contradiction
Zvi Mark's emphasizes that silence and melody are not sequential (present silence → future song) but simultaneous and interpenetrating dimensions of the _zaddik_'s consciousness. The _zaddik_ who is silent on one level is singing on another.
weiss says the zaddik has messianic elements within them
so it is an interiorised messianism to be both silent and to implicate the paradox into oneself and thereby be propelled forward as though giving birth
one must distinguish whether one's problem is of broken vessels or tsimtsum
the first can be tackled with thought, the second by embodying the nature of the paradox itself and thereby embodying something of infinitude: if the capacity for finitude is coiled within infinitude then the capacity for infinitude may be coiled within finitude? in any case one might infold something "other" in the scream
the same should be possible for any art form: what one must do is to implicate the paradox within one's work. this is not a literal contradiction, though it could be, but more the frustration of resolution and continual and repeated infolding of the question - repeated non-resolution
