Implication: the condition of being folded into something, so that neither [[3. Concepts/Turning Around\|re—]]mains the same.

The means of implication are not replicable. Methodology depends on the constitution of that which folds. However, consider sticking a finger in a wound, in an orifice, or folding fluffy, puffy egg whites with sugar. Try accessing something without yourself, or crossing a threshold, or accessing something from [[1. Surface Texts/with-in\|within]]
