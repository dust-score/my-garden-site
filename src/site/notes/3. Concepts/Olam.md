 ע-ל-ם (ʿ-l-m)

עוֹלָם (_olam_) — "world," "eternity," "cosmos"

 עָלַם (_alam_) — "to hide," "to conceal"


_"The fundamental flaw of everything in olam is finitude, the consequence of which is incompleteness and/or inconsistency."_
