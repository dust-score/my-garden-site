In 2021 I was possessed by a question, something along the lines of the quality of delineation of an image from its originary source. I resolved it by removing an aquarium’s worth of water from a lake and placing it so that they faced each other. When I returned a few weeks later, the aquarium had come to life and was populated by small shrimp and plants. I was very relieved.
