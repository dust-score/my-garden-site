_The beginning joins the end in accord with the aspect that is rectified in the middle through this conjunction. For you  have already heard that the unity is revealed intermittently in the rectification of the middle, and through this aspect the beginning and the end are joined, and everything is perfected in one secret in perfection._[^1]

[^1]:  Luzzatto, Ginzei Ramḥal, 264, 268.
