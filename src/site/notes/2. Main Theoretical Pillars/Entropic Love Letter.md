In _[[5. Citations/6. Papers/“Tikkun” Viewed from the Perspective of Systems PhilosophyPhilosophy Martin Zwick\|Tikkun Viewed from the Perspective of Systems Philosophy]]_, Martin Zwick shows that finitude, expressed in [[1. Surface Texts/On Creation\|Lurianic Kabbalah]] as the breaking of the [[3. Concepts/shevirat ha-kelim\|vessels]], is a necessary prerequisite for the existence of such a complex system as life on [[3. Concepts/Olam\|earth]]. Without the [[2. Main Theoretical Pillars/bielik-robson-apocalypse\|flaw]] experienced as [[1. Surface Texts/The Dead End\|The Dead End]], there could be no motion, therefore there could only be stasis: [[5. Citations/6. Papers/The Culture of the End, Tarrying with the Apocalypse, Agata Bielik-Robson\|the eternal eschaton]]. It is therefore an interesting tension that life requires entropy, requires its own limitation, and that this existence within a limited state gives rise to the necessity of [[1. Surface Texts/TIKKUN\|TIKKUN]] - and yet that Tikkun is conceptualised as actualising the [[3. Concepts/Eschaton\|eschaton]], the cessation of [[3. Concepts/On Finitude\|finitude]] and therefore of motion within time, ergo (mortal, physical) life itself. 

Imagining the actualisation of the eschaton, too, implies the concatenation of potentially oppositional conceptions of Tikkun, as it occurs by means of intensification[^1], which may run along [[1. Surface Texts/Jobcenter Texts/anti-accelerationist-infolding\|interiorised]] or exteriorised matrices, with varying degrees of interrelation between the two. Hereby we arrive at divergent implications in regards to the nature of [[1. Surface Texts/TIKKUN\|Tikkun]]: when exteriorised as the actualisation of earthly conditions prerequisite for the arrival of the Messiah, Tikkun can mean something wholly different than when understood as the [[2. Main Theoretical Pillars/Theory Notes/wolfson-time-becoming\|realisation]] of the [[2. Main Theoretical Pillars/Theory Notes/wolfson-repetition-difference\|reality]] of the [[2. Main Theoretical Pillars/wolfson-retroactive-not-yet\|Eschaton]] within the [[3. Concepts/agamben-messianicity-intro\|self]].
