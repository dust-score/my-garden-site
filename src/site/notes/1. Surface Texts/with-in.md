the moment the body ceases being a body but doesn’t stop being a self; Look inside it and turn it inside out, it’s different from every perspective so what about from the inside? Every body is foreign from the inside, stops being body. every face is foreign from the outside. When I look into a face I see hills and caves, and sweat across them to find You.
