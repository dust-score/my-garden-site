I can't help but speculate that I maintain what is basically a monument to bare minimum, the distraction from what's really going on up ahead. This place is built to deny you, and it denies everyone, not only its supplicants. _Just right_ is just shy of bare minimum; the only reason we don't receive it is because someone has got to define the bottom of the barrel, to constitute the limits of societal interiority.
