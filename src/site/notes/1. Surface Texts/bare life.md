_Bare Life_

The protagonist of this book is bare life, that is, the life of _homo sacer_ (sacred man), who may be killed and yet not sacrificed.[^2]

Bare life remains included in politics in the form of the exception, that is, as something that is included solely through an exclusion.[^1]

[^1]: Agamben, _Homo Sacer,_ 11.

[^2]:  Agamben, _Homo Sacer_, 8.
