HEARING [[3. Concepts/reshimu\|RESIDUES]], seeing residues, in 
ward one thousand and one,  

day and night 
the bear polka  

they are reschooling you,  

you are becoming 
he again.

Amos Weisz, translation 

_HÖRRESTE, SEHRESTE, im  
Schlafsaal eintausendundeins,_

_tagnächtlich  
die Bären-Polka:_

_sie schulen dich um,_

_du wirst wieder  
er._

Paul Celan
