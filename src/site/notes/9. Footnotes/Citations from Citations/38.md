We reproduce Klee’s drawing in Théorie de l’art moderne, 73. After the brief presentation on the point in Klee, Deleuze will use this drawing in The Fold: Leibniz and the Baroque, trans. Tom Conley (Minneapolis: University of Minnesota Press, 1993), 15, as illustration of the concept of inflexion in Leibniz, that is, as a genetic element of the fold.
